Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git is extremely useful for working in teams. Multiple users can work on the same project in their own 
branch and then push their changes once they are ready.


Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science could be applied to marketing. Companies rely on big data to determine how to design their ads to appeal
to certain demographics and boost their sales.



Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging creates a new merge commit that combines the changes in the branches being merged while rebasing creates a new 
set of commits that are put on top of the target branch.
